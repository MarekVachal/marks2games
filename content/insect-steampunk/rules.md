## What are these rules?

These are the core rules of the Insect Steampunk tabletop RPG — the system that supports character creation, progression, specialization, and play within this unusual setting.

The rules are designed specifically for the world of Insect Steampunk rather than adapted from a generic fantasy system. Their purpose is to support a style of play that matches the setting’s distinctive atmosphere, societies, and conflicts.

---

## What makes these rules different?

Insect Steampunk is built as a custom RPG system developed together with the setting itself.

Instead of relying only on familiar fantasy assumptions, the system is intended to support:

- structured character growth
- meaningful specialization
- distinctive archetypes
- a setting-driven style of play
- mechanics that fit an unusual world rather than simply borrowing from conventional fantasy templates

The intention is to create a game where system and world reinforce one another.
