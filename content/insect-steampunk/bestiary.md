## Bestiary

The **Bestiary** collects creatures, enemies, and other beings that inhabit the world of Insect Steampunk.

It is intended to help game masters and players better understand the strange lifeforms, dangers, and living presences that shape this setting.

---

## What to expect

The Bestiary is meant to contain:

- hostile and neutral creatures
- unusual insect-inspired beings
- enemies suitable for encounters and campaigns
- creatures that reflect the world’s atmosphere and ecology
- material that expands the setting beyond the player perspective

As the project develops, this collection may grow into a broader world resource rather than remaining only a combat reference.

---

## Project status

The Bestiary is part of an evolving RPG project, so entries and classifications may continue to change over time.
