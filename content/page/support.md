## Support Marks2Games

Marks2Games is an independent studio project built out of enthusiasm for games, worldbuilding, and original mechanics.

If you would like to support our work, you can do so through Buy Me a Coffee:

**Buy Me a Coffee:** https://buymeacoffee.com/gravitygame?l=en

Your support helps us invest more time and resources into development, writing, design, testing, and future releases.

## What Your Support Helps With

By supporting Marks2Games, you help us continue working on:

- **Gravity Game** — our sci-fi online strategy game for Android
- **Insect Steampunk** — our pen-and-paper RPG project
- **Gladiators** — our modern card-based board game in development

Support also helps with general production costs such as art, tools, publishing, playtesting, and long-term development.

## Why Support Matters

We want to create games that are imaginative, mechanically interesting, and fair to players. We are especially committed to avoiding pay-to-win design and making games that people can enjoy on their own terms.

Every form of support helps us move a little further.

## Thank You

Whether you support us financially, share our work, or simply follow our projects, thank you for helping Marks2Games grow.
