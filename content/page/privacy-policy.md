Depending on how you use our services, we may collect the following types of information.

If you sign in to **Gravity Game** using Google authentication, we may collect:

- your display name
- your email address
- your profile picture

If you use a guest account, an anonymous account identifier may be created instead.

### Gameplay and account data

We may collect and store gameplay-related information such as:

- game progress
- in-game statistics
- matchmaking or multiplayer-related data
- account settings and preferences

This data is used to make the game function properly and to improve the player experience.

### Technical and device information

We may also collect non-personal technical information such as:

- device type
- operating system version
- app version
- performance and crash-related information
- general usage analytics

This helps us improve stability, compatibility, and overall user experience.

We may use collected information for the following purposes:
